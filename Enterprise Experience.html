Innovative Global Solutions Enterprise

I have been a member of the Innovative Global Solutions enterprise on both the ventilator team as well as the Stryker team. This enterprise focuses on creating solutions for underdeveloped countries and underserved areas. The ventilator team is focusing on creating a low-cost portable ventilator that can be used in countries such as Ghana. My work for the Ventilator Team includes creating an instruction sheet and user manual for the ventilator, creating advertising posters for the enterprise, designing the final expo poster and updating the website.
